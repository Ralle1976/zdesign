## VARIANT 1

Design the 20% that makes a page unmistakably alive — not the 80% any model ships by default.

- One bold, ownable visual move: a 4-column treatment grid (repeat(4,1fr)) where at least one card is full-bleed image with overlay text — never the symmetric 6-up template-portfolio layout. One dark-bg testimonial card with a gold accent edge, never scale(1.04)+border. Break the grid on purpose.
- Display face at 600 with letter-spacing -.03em and clamp(48px,7vw,84px) — 500 reads anemic at this scale. Italic for one word of contrast, weight elsewhere.
- Real microcopy voice: headlines read like one specific human wrote them for one specific audience — sharp, sensory, specific. No "Unlock your potential." If a line could belong to any spa, rewrite it.
- One detail only a real user adds: a specific treatment number, an honest contraindication, copy that signals someone lives here.

Test: identifiable from a screenshot. Cover the logo — if it could be any wellness brand, start over.
